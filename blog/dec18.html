Daily Prayer for Courage

Joshua 1:9, ESV, "Have I not commanded you? Be strong and courageous. Do not be frightened, and do not be dismayed, for the LORD your GOD is with you wherever you go."

Feel free to change/add anything to this prayer!

"Lord, Father in Heaven, I ask You in prayer today to give me an opportunity for Confidence. Lord let me shine my light, and let me stand tall, bringing glory to Your Almighty Name. In Jesus name we pray, Amen."

This prayer is perfect for both small and large obstacles in life. Surrendering your anxieties and fears to God and letting him guide you can help you face anything with confidence and courage.
